6–9 seconds
Technicians perform proper gowning, hand hygiene, and aseptic technique before entering the ISO 5 cleanroom. Close-up shots emphasize sterile procedures.

9–12 seconds
The PIC reviews environmental monitoring reports and classified area certification documents on a tablet while observing cleanroom operations.

18–21 seconds
A customer complaint appears on the compliance dashboard. The PIC investigates, reviews documentation, determines root cause, and records corrective actions.

21–24 seconds

24–27 seconds
The pharmacy team works efficiently while the PIC confidently oversees operations and coaches staff.

27–30 seconds
Closing cinematic shot of the pharmacy with the message: "Leadership Through Compliance. Excellence Through Accountability."
